**Research Objectives:**

1. Evaluate the accuracy of AI transcription systems in Portuguese-language focus groups with 8 to 10 speakers.
2. Measure the impact of overlapping speech, interruptions, noise, accents, and informal language on transcription quality.
3. Compare speaker diarization performance across different transcription backends.
4. Analyze the reliability of timestamps and segment-level speaker attribution.
5. Identify common failure modes, including speaker confusion, omitted speech, hallucinated words, broken punctuation, and loss of short interventions.
6. Develop a reproducible pipeline for audio preprocessing, chunking, transcription, diarization, transcript cleaning, and qualitative reporting.
7. Explore AI-assisted sentiment and discourse analysis, including emotional intensity, frustration, confidence, disagreement, consensus, and thematic salience.
8. Produce anonymized examples, benchmark metrics, documentation, and open-source tools for qualitative researchers.

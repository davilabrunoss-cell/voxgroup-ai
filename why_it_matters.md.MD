**Potential Impact:**

Focus group transcription is a costly and time-consuming task, especially in public opinion research, social diagnosis, market research, and policy evaluation. Human transcription can take several hours for each hour of audio, and the difficulty increases significantly when multiple people speak at the same time.

AI transcription can reduce cost and processing time, but its limitations are not yet well understood in complex real-world group settings, especially in Brazilian Portuguese.

This project will help researchers understand how AI systems perform under realistic qualitative research conditions. It will also provide practical tools and guidelines for combining automated transcription with human review, improving productivity while preserving methodological rigor.

The project may benefit research institutes, universities, public policy teams, social scientists, and organizations working with interviews, focus groups, participatory research, and community listening.
